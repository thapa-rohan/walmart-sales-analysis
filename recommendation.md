# Walmart Sales Performance — Business Case Analysis
### Analyst: Rohan Thapa | Date: April 2026

---

## Executive Summary
Analysis of 10,051 Walmart transactions reveals a critical misalignment 
between sales volume and customer satisfaction. High-revenue categories 
are underperforming on quality, while high-quality categories are 
underperforming on sales — representing a significant growth opportunity.

---

## Key Findings

**Finding 1 — Quality crisis in top-selling category**
Home and lifestyle generates the highest revenue ($493,628) but has 
the lowest customer rating of all categories (5.74/10). This suggests 
customers are buying out of necessity but leaving dissatisfied — 
a retention risk.

**Finding 2 — Undervalued high-performers**
Food and beverages has the highest customer rating (7.11/10) and best 
profit margin (0.40) but ranks 4th in total sales ($53,471). This 
category is significantly under-promoted relative to its quality.

**Finding 3 — Geographic concentration**
Sales are heavily concentrated in Weslaco ($46,645) and Waxahachie 
($40,966), suggesting untapped potential in lower-performing cities.

---

## Recommendations

**Recommendation 1 — Improve product quality in Home and lifestyle**
Conduct customer surveys to identify specific pain points. 
Replace underperforming SKUs with higher-rated alternatives.
Target: Improve category rating from 5.74 to 7.0+ within 2 quarters.

**Recommendation 2 — Invest in Food and beverages marketing**
Increase shelf space and promotional budget for Food and beverages.
Its high margin (0.40) means more revenue per dollar sold.
Target: Grow category sales by 30% through targeted promotions.

**Recommendation 3 — Expand top-performing city strategies**
Replicate the sales strategies used in Weslaco and Waxahachie 
across lower-performing cities to close the geographic gap.

---

## Conclusion
Walmart can significantly grow revenue by fixing the quality gap 
in its top-selling category and scaling its highest-rated category. 
These two changes alone could add an estimated $150,000+ in annual revenue.

---
*Analysis conducted using Python (Pandas, Matplotlib, Seaborn) 
on a dataset of 10,051 transactions across Texas locations.*
